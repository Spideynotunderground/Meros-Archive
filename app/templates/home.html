About Meros

Preserving the wisdom of generations

Our Mission

The Meros Archive is dedicated to capturing and preserving the invaluable stories, wisdom, and cultural heritage of Uzbekistan's elders before they are lost to time.

Starting from the historic city of Bukhara, we are building a comprehensive digital repository that celebrates the diverse voices and experiences that have shaped our cultural landscape.
